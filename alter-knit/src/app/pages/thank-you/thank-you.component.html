Preparing your Garment for Shipping
  1. Please DO NOT put tape anywhere on your garment(s) to indicate holes. As part of your service with us, we will thoroughly examine the item for all damages.
  2. Please DO NOT clean your garment(s) before submitting. Any cleaning process will make the hole or holes bigger incurring you additional costs.
  3. If you need an item to be rewoven and it is a part of a suit, please provide us with both pieces.
  4. Due to the heavy amount of orders we receive and limited studio space, please DO NOT include any hangers, hanging bags, or reusable bags that you want returned as we cannot store them for you while you are in line for repair.
